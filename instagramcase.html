According to the Pew Research Center, social media usage is increasing in demographics with lower education levels and low income. This has shown me, despite social media always being on trial for fostering depression, its wide reach can provide opportunities for use in prevention of mental illnesses in users from low income and low education backgrounds. For my individual project, I would like to work on creating a responsive design of mental health check point to be incorporated into a social media application called Instagram. This project is not yet finished.
